Spring 2016 Commencement and Recognition Ceremonies

Spring 2016 Commencement and Recognition Ceremonies The Spring ECU Commencement Ceremony is scheduled for Friday, May 6, 2016 in Dowdy-Ficklen Stadium at 9:00 a.m. Degree candidates and faculty who will participate are expected to make a reservation via the ECU Commencement web page. That web page also provides further information about the ECU ceremony.

The College of Health and Human Performance invites graduates, families, and friends to help celebrate the accomplishments of those receiving degrees. The HHP Graduate Recognition Ceremony will be held at 12:00pm on Saturday, May 7th in Williams Arena at Minges Coliseum. Tickets are not required for entrance and ample parking is available at Minges Coliseum. If you have questions regarding the HHP ceremony, please call 252-328-4630.

HHP graduates in academic regalia are asked to be in line by degree and department in the coliseum hallways of Minges no later than 11:30am on Saturday, May 7th. Signs will be posted to assist in identifying the correct line up locations as well as student marshals and staff.

A professional photographer will take a picture of each graduate as they cross the stage. Visit the link for more information aboutgraduation photos.
