HHP Scholarships and Endowments

The College of Health & Human Performance offers scholarships that are available to any major within the college, as well as scholarships that are specific to individual departments and majors within the College of Health & Human Performance.

  • Students must be seeking a degree within the College of Health & Human Performance and must remain within the academic degree for which the scholarship is awarded.
  • As a condition of accepting the scholarship, students are required to attend the Winners Circle Breakfast award ceremony.
  • All scholarships require that students be enrolled full-time during the terms for which the scholarship is awarded. Failure to do so could lead to loss of scholarship.

For a complete listing of all HHP departmental and college-wide opportunities please click on the link below and type in the search box "College of Health and Human Performance" Please note that attempting to search scholarships by major or degree program may result in an incomplete listing of all opportunities. https://ecu.academicworks.com

Unless otherwise noted, application deadline for scholarships is January 31
Scholarship awards are subject to funding availability
